The story of how we got started

With over 100 state-of-the-art CNC machines, during pandemic times we decided to start manufacturing our own products:

March 2020
Health first. We opened our medical division. Astreea® became the largest global manufacturer of pedal dispensers, with over 250.000 units produced in the first 4 months, helping reduce the spread of Covid-19 at a global scale.
November 2020
Lifestyle. We developed our first lifestyle product, the Astreea® Igloo. A great solution for outdoor dining, glamping, and rediscovering the simple pleasures in life. The beauty of life hides in all season, so we’ve created 6 different covers for our Igloo.
see products

“If initially the Astreea® Igloo has been designed as a solution for outdoor dinning  during covid times, it turned out to be much more. Today, the Igloo it’s a magical product that brings joy to hundred of thousands of people on all continents.”

Andrei Ghinea
Co-founder and CEO of Astreea

February 2021
Meet me Downtown. We developed the Astreea® Downtown, an outdoor solution for restaurants with very limited space, or for luxury greenhouses.

Supporting Ukraine. Together with UNICEF, we’ve created the Astreea® Bed, a solution for refugees fleeing the war.

Out on the Terrace. We launched the Astreea® Terrace, a versatile solution completing the outdoor dining setting.

July 2022
Astreea® opened its green mobility division with the launch of the EV chargers for electrical and plug-in hybrid cars.

26 different models of EV chargers are under this range, covering any DC solution of up to 22KW, from stainless steel structure self-standing chargers to wallboxes.
