FAQ - MEDICAL
Everything you want to know
How do I know when the bottle is empty?

Each dispenser has a slit where you can see the level of the sanitizer inside the bottle.

Do you provide Astreea® sanitizer with your dispenser?

Yes, we do have our own sanitizer, which you can purchase separately if you choose to.

Is it safe to use your dispenser outdoor?

Yes, the dispenser is made out of medical grade stainless steel and the drive is purely mechanical, no electronic/electric parts included.

How do I refill the bottle?

Using the Allen key provided with the dispenser, remove the lid and take out the sanitizer bottle and refill to your own liking.

What kind of sanitizer can be used: gel or liquid?

The pump of the dispenser works best if used with gel sanitizer. You can also use liquid sanitizer, but it may cause splashing, which can be inconvenient.

Is the dispenser customizable?

Yes, upon request we can laser etch or paint your brand’s logo on the dispenser.
